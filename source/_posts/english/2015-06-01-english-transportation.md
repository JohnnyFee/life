## 交通工具（vehicles）

- ride a bike / by bike / bike to work
- go by car
- ride the streetcar 乘坐有轨电车
- take the train
- take a taxi
- take the bus
- go on foot
- take the subway
- go by plane
- boat
- go by ferry 搭乘轮渡

请使用 go by + 交通工具或者 get there by + 交通工具指路。

- You should go by bus.
- You can get to the park by subway.
- You can get there by subway.

您也可以说 take + the + 交通工具。

- Take the bus. It's raining. 
- You can take the train to the beach.

告诉您信息的人会让您知道您是否理解正确。

- Correct
- Yes, that's right.

公交车通常用数字（number）表示。地铁和火车通常用线路（line）表示。线路可以用名字或者数字表示。

- Take the number 17 bus to the subway station. 地铁站
- You're going there by subway? Take the blue line.   您坐地铁去那吗？坐蓝线
- Then change to bus number 7 there.
- The university is the fourth or fifth stop.

告诉他们应该在哪站上下车，或者哪站换乘不同公交或地铁，这是有帮助的。

- Get on Line 4. 上 4 号线。
- Change at the university to Line 2. 在大学换 2 号线。
- Get off at the second stop. 在第二站下车。
- Get off at (the) Pine Street.

## 街道

街道名称有多个不同的单词。以下是一些关键词和相应的缩写形式。缩写多用于文件、新建和邮件中正式地址的书写。如果与具体街名搭配使用，记住街道单词首字母一定要大写，比如 Fourth Avenue。

- street = St.    街
- road = Rd.  路
- avenue = Ave.   大街
- boulevard = Blvd.   大道
- drive = Dr. 道
- way 路

block 是指街道从一个街角到另一个街角的某个部分，在指路时常常使用。

- Go down Main Street for three blocks, and then make a right.    沿 Main Street 街走三个街区，然后右转。
- Go south for two blocks, and turn left. 往南走两个街区，然后左转。

使用 on 谈论某个地方位于的街道

I live on Maple Road.   我住在 Maple Road 路。

The library is on Pine Street.  图书馆位于 Pine Street 街。

使用 at 谈论街道的具体号码。

I live at 1215(12 15) Maple Road.                      我住在 Maple Road 路 1215 号。              
The library is at 57 Pine Street.   图书馆位于 Pine Street 街 57 号。
         
街道号码有多种不同的念法。如果号码为一两个数字，可按一般的方法来念。

The bank is at 43 Central Boulevard.    银行位于 Central Boulevard 大道 43 号。

如果有三到四个数字，念法就有几种。

- I live at 570(5 70) Beverly Drive.                我住在 Beverly Drive 道 570 号。       
- I live at 1215(1 2 1 5) Maple Road.  我住在 Maple Road 路 1215 号。
- The theater is at 406(4 0 6) Sunshine Way. 电影院位于 Sunshine Way 路 406 号。

如果数字多于四个，那将每个数字念出来。

- The bank is at 11743 Fifth Avenue.      银行位于 Fifth Avenue 大街 11743 号。

注意如果数字是 0，你可以念做 zero 或者 oh。

## 问路

- How do I get to the museum?  
- How do I get there?
- What number bus?
- How long does it take by bus?
- Do you know a good place for coffee?
- Do you know a nice park near here?  
- Excuse/Pardon me. Can you help me? 
- Can you please tell me how to get there?

被问话人可能需要了解你的更多信息，比如：

- Where are you now?                  你现在在哪？            
- How are you coming? 你怎么来？
- Where are you coming from?

使用下列问题询问某人居住的地方或某个地位的地址：
 
- A: Where do you live?
- B: I live on Pine Street.  
         
- A: What's your address?
- B: It's 507 Pine Street. 
         
- A: What's the address of the restaurant?
- B: The restaurant's at 819 Ocean Avenue.

Drive north up Third Avenue for two blocks. Then make a left onto Sixth Street. Our building's on the right, at 46 Sixth Street.

在得知地址后，你可能需要询问以下后续问题。
 
横街：

- A: What's the cross street? 横街是什么？(与哪条路交叉？)
- B: The cross street is Fourth Avenue.   横街是 Fourth 大道。

公寓：

- A: What's your apartment number? 你的公寓号码是多少？
- B: It's 2314.   2314 室。

楼层：

- A: What floor do you live on? 你住在几楼？
- B: The 23rd floor.  23 楼。

## 指路

使用下列表达告诉他人沿街道直行：
 
- Go straight on Pine Avenue. 沿 Pine Avenue 大街直走。
- Go down Oak Street.         沿 Oak Street 街直行。

使用表达 turn right 和 turn left 指示方向。这些表达后接 on。

- Go straight on Pine Avenue. Then turn right on Central Boulevard.   沿 pine Avenue 大街直行，然后在 Central Boulevard 大道右转。
- Go down Oak Street. Next, turn left on Valley Road. 沿 Oak Steet 街直行，在 Valley Road 路左转。

你也可以使用这些表达告诉他人右转或左转：
 
- Go right on Central Boulevard.  在 Central Boulevard 大道右转。
- Make a left on Valley Road. 在 Valley Road 路左转。

要表示某个地方位于街道的右边或左边，使用以下表达：
 
- His house is on the right.  他的房子在右边。
- The building is on the left.    大楼在左边。

重复路线确认时，你可以使用 so 一词。
 
- A: Go down Main Street, and then make a right on Terrace Drive.
- B: So, I go down Main and make a right on Terrace.
- A: Yes, that's right.

为你指路的人会告诉你是否理解正确。

- Correct.    对的。
- Uh-huh. 嗯。
- Yes, that's right.  对，没错。

Take the number 13 bus, and get off  at Pine Street. Then walk north  on First Street for three blocks  . Our office is on the left  at 3489. We're on the fourth floor  in office number  420.

Our building is on the right, 1234 avenue. Our office is on the second floor, number 005.

### 行驶时间

请使用动词 take 谈论行驶的时间。

- How long does it take by bus?   坐公交车需要多久呢？
- It takes about 20 to 30 minutes..

### 方位

四个罗经方位分别为 north (N)、south (S)、east (E) 和 west (W)，指路时常常使用这四个词。

- Go north on Third Avenue.   沿 Third Avenue 大街往北走。
- Go down Central Boulevard, and turn east on Clover Street.  沿 Central Boulevard 大道直行，然后在 Clover Street 街转东。

你也可以用 which 询问方位的问题。

- A: Which direction is it?
- B: It's west. 

其他访问

- across the street 街对面

    使用 across the street 表示某场所在街对面

    The bookstore is across the street.

    across the street from 则表示某场所与另一场所隔街对望。

    The bookstore is across the street from the restaurant. 书店与餐厅隔街对望。
 
- on the corner of 在街角

    使用 on the corner of + 两条街道的名称表示两条街道交汇的地方。

    The bank is on the corner of Pine Street and First Street.

- next to 在旁边 / The library is next to the playground.
- in front of 在前面

    使用 in front of 表示某场所位于另一场所的前方。

    The bus stop is in front of the supermarket.

- near here 在附近
- 使用 between + 两个场所的名称表示某场所在这两个场所之间。

    The post office is between the restaurant and the movie theater.

- 使用 behind 表示某场所在另一场所的后面。

    The pizza place is behind the library.

## 介绍城市或小镇的位置

Hometown 是指你出生或长大的地方。

- A: What's your hometown?    哪里是你的家乡？
- B: It's New York City.  纽约市。

如果某人不熟悉你的家乡，他可能问你的家乡在哪里。使用介词 on 搭配以下地理词汇。注意在此处，on 与 next to 的意思相同。

- It's on the ocean.  在海边。
- It's on a lake. 在湖边。
- It's on the beach.  在海滩上。
- It's on a river.    在河边。
- It's on the coast.  在海岸上。

使用介词 in 搭配以下地理词汇。注意在此处，in 与 in the middle of 的意思相同。

- It's in a forest.   在森林里。
- It's in the mountains.  在山里。
- It's in the hills.  在山里。
- It's in a valley.   在山谷里。
- It's in the desert. 在沙漠里。

说明一座城市的位置时，您也可以使用介词 in 搭配指南针的四个方向：north、south、east 和 west。

- It's in the north.  在北边。
- It's in the south.  在南边。
- It's in the east.   在东边。
- It's in the west.   在西边。

询问某个地点再某个地方的位置：

- Where __in Brazil__ is Rio de Janeiro?
- Rio de Janeiro is a big city in the south of Brazil. It's on the east coast of the country. 

Rio de Janeiro is a big city in the south  of Brazil. It's on the east coast  of the country. Rio has beautiful beaches  on the Atlantic Ocean  . Two very famous beaches are Ipanema and Copacabana. Rio is also in  the mountains. Sugarloaf is the name of a very famous mountain  in Rio.

## 距离

使用表达 How far is it? 询问到某地的距离。
 
- A: Let's go to the coffee shop.          
- B: How far is it?
- A: It's only two blocks.

询问两地之间的距离，使用表达 How far is it from (place) to (place)?

- A: How far is it from your house to the beach?
- B: It's about two kilometers.

世界上大多数地方都使用 kilometers (km) 衡量距离。英国和美国使用 miles，1 英里约等于 1.6 公里。如果你不知道确切数量，可以在数字前使用 around 或 about。
